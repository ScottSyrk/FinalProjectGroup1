Peachy Apple Sangria


Ingredients

  • Half of a medium sized apple, chopped into small pieces
  • Half of an orange, sliced into small pieces after removing seeds
  • One peach, sliced into small pieces, after removing core (keep some to Garnish)
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 cup orange juice
  • half a cup of brandy
  • a bottle of red wine of choice

In a large bowl, mix all the chopped apples, oranges, and peaches. Then add wine, orange juice, and brandy. Slowly incorporate brown sugar until you reach preferred taste. You can chill the mixture in the fridge for a few hours until it reaches the desired cool temperature.
