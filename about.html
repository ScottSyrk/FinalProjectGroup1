About Us

A Select FEW is a unique Bar + Spirits service. Our physical bar is located at the Troy Innovation Garage, 24 Fourth Street, Troy, NY 12180. Enjoy wine, champagne, bourbon and soda cocktails in a relaxed atmosphere. Our Select Flights offer two cocktails with premium, artisanal ingredients and only the best, select spirits. The soda mocktails are a great choice if you prefer a non-alcoholic drink.

  • Wine Flights
  • Bourbon Flights
  • Champagne Flights
  • Soda Flights
If you can't join us in person, you are welcome to order our unique packages online - We ship throughout the continental United States.
