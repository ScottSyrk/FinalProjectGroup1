Champagne Cocktail


Ingredients

  • 1 sugar cube
  • 5 dashes Angostura bitters
  • squeezed lemon peel
  • 1 glass of champagne
  • Lemon twist, for garnish

Add a sugar cube to bottom of a champagne flute. Add 5 dashes of bitters. Use a vegetable peeler to peel off a small strip of lemon skin. Squeeze the lemon skin into the flute to release the oils. Fill the glass with champagne. Garnish with a lemon twist.
